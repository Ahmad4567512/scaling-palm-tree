About the Planets

The solar system has eight planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter,
Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. There are five officially recognized dwarf
planets in our solar system: Ceres, Pluto, Haumea, Makemake, and Eris.



Inner Planets

The first four planets from the Sun are Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars. These inner planets also are known as terrestrial
planets because they have solid surfaces.
